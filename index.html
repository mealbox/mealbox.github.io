About Mealbox

What Mealbox Represents


The MealBox is a branded vending machine, which dispenses affordable wholesome healthy meals. The first row serves breakfast items, the second row serves lunch items, the mid row serve dinner items, the bottom row consists of desserts, and finally, the last row will contain beverages. These vending machines are built extremely high tech with built in heating and cooling capabilities. The heat from the cooling process from the energy-saving thermal technology is recycled to heat the warm products. This allows meals to be dispensed warm and ready to be eaten on the spot. Also, it allows cool drinks and desserts to go along with the meals. Our vending machines will have LED screen with video and voice capabilities. Videos will be playing during the waiting time while the food is getting prepared. However, if our consumers prefer, they can use the video game capabilities offered in selective vending machines during the wait time. These vending machines bring value to consumers as they have quick access to wholesome healthy meals 24/7, prepared in minutes.
